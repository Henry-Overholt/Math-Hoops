Welcome to Math Hoops the Computer Game!

Math Hoops is a fast-paced math game, designed to teach you probability, as well as quick decisions. You will practice quick mental math skills with single digit adding, subtract, multiplying, and division. You will use real life statistics of their favorite WNBA and NBA players, to see if YOU can beat the computer!
