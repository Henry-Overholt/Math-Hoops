Welcome to Math Hoops the Computer Game!

Math Hoops is a fast-paced math game, designed to teach students probability, as well as quick decisions. They will practice their quick mental math skills with single digit, adding, subtract, multiplying, and division. Players use real life statistics of their favorite WNBA and NBA players, to see if they can beat the computer!
