How to play...

  1. When you hit Start Game the game clock will start as well as your play clock. The game lasts 15 minutes, and you have 30 seconds per turn.
  2. During your turn, you roll the dice. Then with the two numbers, you add them, subtract them (always the bigger number first), you multiply them, and you divide them if they are even.
  3. Once you submit the answers, you look at where you on your side of the board you can shoot from. Meaning what "even" or "odd" numbers you calculated are available to shot from.
  4. Select your shot and see if your player of that color made the shot or not!
