How the Draft works!

  1. Click the Flip for the First Pick! Button to decided if you go first or the computer picks first!
  2. You must select one player from each color ( red , green, orange, blue, and purple), and after you pick, the computer picks makes its selection.
  3. Once you have all five players, the draft will end and you will be able to go to the game!
